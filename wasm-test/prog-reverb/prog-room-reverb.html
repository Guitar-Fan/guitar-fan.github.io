About ProG Room Reverb
ProG is a warm, natural room reverb designed for professional audio production. It features carefully crafted early reflections, warm all-pass filtering, and smooth room-characteristic comb filters that simulate the intimate acoustics of recording studios and natural spaces with exceptional warmth and ambience.
